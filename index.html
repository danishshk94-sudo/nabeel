My Story

I'm Hamza Mesia—a self-taught graphic designer and creative professional with over 5 years of experience in branding, graphic design, print production, video animations, and web design. What started as a passion for art and visual communication has grown into a full-service creative studio.

I believe great design is more than aesthetics—it's about solving problems, telling stories, and creating connections. Whether it's a startup needing a complete brand identity or a business looking for eye-catching marketing materials, I bring the same level of dedication and creativity to every project.

Based in Morocco, I work with clients locally and internationally. My approach is personal, collaborative, and always focused on delivering work that exceeds expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

It depends on the project scope. A logo design typically takes 3–5 days, a full brand identity 1–2 weeks, print materials 3–7 days, and a website 2–4 weeks. I'll give you an exact timeline after our initial discussion.

Every project is custom-quoted based on scope. Logo design starts at $100, brand identity packages at $300, social media graphics at $50/piece, print materials at $80, video animations at $150, and websites at $500. Contact me for a personalized quote.

I include 2–3 rounds of revisions in every package depending on the project type. My goal is to get it right through clear communication from the start, so most clients are happy with minimal revisions.

You'll receive all file formats you need: AI, EPS, SVG, PDF for print; PNG, JPG, WebP for digital; MP4 for video. Print files include proper bleed, CMYK color, and trim marks. Source files are always included.

I deliver print-ready files with all the correct specifications. While I don't handle physical printing directly, I can recommend trusted printing partners and coordinate with them on your behalf to ensure the best quality.
