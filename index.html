Our Story

Founded in 2016, Bravo started as a two-person operation in a Brooklyn co-working space. We believed that the best design comes from genuine partnerships—not transactional relationships.

Today, we're a team of 14 designers, developers, and strategists based in New York City, working with clients across six continents. We've grown, but our philosophy hasn't changed: every brand deserves work that's crafted with intention and built to last.

We don't do templates. We don't do shortcuts. We do work we're proud of—and that our clients are proud to own.
